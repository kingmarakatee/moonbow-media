Privacy Policy (GDPR)

Last updated: March 4, 2026

1. Commitment to Privacy

Moonbow Universe respects your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, protect and manage your personal data, in compliance with the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. What data do we collect?

Through our website (contact form, newsletter subscription and communication via email/WhatsApp), we may collect:

  • First and last name.
  • Email address.
  • Phone / WhatsApp number.
  • Information about events and project needs.
  • Images and videos captured during the provision of our audiovisual and broadcasting services.

3. How do we use your data?

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Responding to quote requests and providing the requested information.
  • Executing and managing the audiovisual service you contracted.
  • Billing and legal accounting matters.
  • Occasionally sending you our newsletter, only if you have explicitly subscribed.

4. Data Sharing

Moonbow Universe does not sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties. We may share data only with partners strictly necessary for business operations (e.g., web hosting services, billing software) that also comply with GDPR.

5. Security & Storage

We adopt appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration or destruction. Video and photography files are stored on secure local hard drives and in the cloud (with restricted access) during the editing process and as backup, being deleted upon the client's express request after the project is completed.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation, as a data subject, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Request the correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request that your information and images be deleted from our records.
  • Portability: Request the transfer of your data to you.
  • Objection / Withdrawal of consent: Tell us to stop using your data (e.g., unsubscribe from the newsletter).

7. Contact

To exercise any of these rights or if you have questions about this policy, please contact us via email: hello@moonbowmedia.pt.
